Denver Broncos' win over Los Angeles Chargers (10/6/2019)

Broncos shut down Chargers in the first half with 17 points. What an exciting game in the City of Angels!

Chargers put some points on the board in the second half. They scored a touchdown and 2 field goals. The Chargers defense kept the Broncos from scoring any more touchdowns in the second half.

Unfortunately for Los Angeles the Broncos scored enough in the first half and played defense well enough to win the game. The final score is Denver 20 and Los Agneles 13. Let's see if the Broncos can do it again at home on December 1st, 2019. Stay Tuned!
